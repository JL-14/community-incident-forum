Welcome to the Reigate Community Issues and Incidents Forum

Information about issues and incidents in and around Reigate, from official sources and residents

Why this site?

Ever wondered what is happening in a particular place or at a particular time? Why the road is closed, whether there is anything to be aware of on a certain route (potholes, overhanging branches, uneven pavements)?
Or maybe you have had a positive or negative experience in a particular location you wish to let others know about?
That is where we come in.

Who this site is for

The Reigate Community Issues and Incidents Forum is your one-stop shop for information about your local area. It is intended for use by residents (and visitors/commuters, etc) to bring together information from a range of official sources about issues and incidents that may affect your day. The aim is to empower residents to make informed decisions about day-to-day activities, identifying potential obstacles for your plans (whether they be train strikes, road closures, or other issues).
The site combines official notices from local authorities, transport services, and law enforcement with locally generated insights and observations.

Experiencing an emergency?

Please note that this forum is NOT linked to the emergency services or to local authorities. If you are experiencing an issue which requires immediate attention or intervention, please call 999 (for emergencies) or the relevant council service!

How to use the site

This forum is maintained by Reigate residents for Reigate residents and visitors. The forum is mediated, and in order to report an incident or issue you need to contact us via the e-mail link on the Contact Us page. In order to comment on issues raised or to provide updates based your observations, you can do this once registered and logged in to the forum.
Every report and comment is reviewed and, where possible, verified. This is to avoid incorrect content from being posted, but may lead to a slight delay between a report or comment being posted and appearing on the forum.

Any questions?

If you have any questions, queries, or suggestions, or want to know more about the forum, please do get in touch through the Contact Us link at the top of the page!
