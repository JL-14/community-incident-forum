How to let us know about issues or incidents that are not covered on the website

If you are aware of something going on in your local area which is not already covered on this website, you can let us know by sending us an e-mail.
Please include information about the issue/ incident, including:

  • A description of the issue or incident
  • Time and date for when it occurred (or is planned)
  • Location (postcode or street name)
  • Whether it is resolved or ongoing (and if ongoing, whether you know when it will end)
  • Whether the issue or incident is urgent, or poses potential risk of harm to those in the locality
  • Whether you have notified the authorities (council, police, others) about it
  • Attach any photos (if relevant or available)

We will review any information about new or ongoing issues, and add them to the website. There may be a slight delay between submitting a new issue/ incident until it appears, due to the review process.
